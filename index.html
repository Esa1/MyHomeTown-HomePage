Top three activities to do at Turku
Take a walk around the city center.
The city center is full of middle-aged beautiful buildings to take a look at while taking an evening walk-around.
Aurajoki flows just in the middle of the city.
Turku has a brilliant library and there's many restaurant to relax in on the shores of Aurajoki.
Meet people at the market place.
The new market place is the best place to stop for a coffee and meet friend and new people. .
